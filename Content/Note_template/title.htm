If your release notes use a single topic file, remove the Note condition from the Contents that use the mini-toc proxy (this is the default). If your release notes use multiple topic files, remove the Note condition from the Contents that use the output toc proxy. Do not use TOC proxies in your online outputs. TOC proxies work differently online and are supported in master pages only for non-print outputs.
